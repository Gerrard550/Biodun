Difference between the Em, b and Strong

The Em element is used basically to add stress emphasis in a text. For example There are many weak buildings in Lagos.

The Strong element is used for text that has a special significance. For example To get the best result in your exam, you must face your studies.

The b element is used to bring attention to a text but with no special significance unlike the Strong element. For example; The Federal ministry of health warns that smokers are liable to die young.

Difference between Inline Element and Block Level Element

The Inline element takes up the little piece of line required for their content, examples of inline elements are em, sup, sub while the Block level elements takes up a full block of space, examples are the paragraphs and headings.
